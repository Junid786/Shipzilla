Shipzilla USA Inc. Terms and Conditions


  1. Shipzilla USA Inc. is a listing service that links together customers with transporters. This is the exclusive service we render and we are not a freight company.

  2. The customer pays a fee to be listed on our website.

  3. Shipzilla's fees are independent of any other fee that could be charged by a Broker to the customer.

  4. Listing fees are determined in each case according to the type of vehicles, boats, RVs and heavy equipment to be transported and distances between pick up and delivery locations.

  5. The customer authorizes Shipzilla USA Inc. to charge his/her credit card upon selection of a transporter

  6. The listing process works as follows:
    1. the customer requests a quote.
    2. the customer fills in all the required information,
    3. quotes are submitted on our website and by email,
    4. the customer compares quotes and accepts the preferred transporter,
    5. the customer pays Shipzilla's deposit,
    6. the customer provides pickup and delivery addresses and names and telephone contact numbers at pick upand delivery locations,
    7. the transporter immediately contacts the customer to schedule a pick up.

  7. Shipzilla's fees are refundable if the transporter fails to pick up as instructed by the customer. Shipzilla USA Inc. liability is limited only to the amount of the listing fee charged. In case of a transporter cancellation by the customer, fees are not refundable.

  8. Shipzilla USA Inc. does not vouch for the accuracy and truthfulness of any information provided by the transporter regarding insurance coverage. It is the responsibiliy of the customer to verify such information.

  9. Shipzilla USA Inc. is not responsible for any pick up or delivery dates and for weather, traffic or mechanical breakdown delays. Shipzilla USA Inc. is not responsible for any storage fees or car rental fees.
